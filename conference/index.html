"INNOVATION"

...is at the tip of everyone's tongue in the rail industry now. A word that was disregarded and thrown away 24 months ago is now the hottest thing since the Stephenson Rocket.

Unfortunately, despite being so heavily used, so little is actually being done in the industry, and what little activity there is second rate compared to other markets and industries.

We're not here to answer how we've ended up here in this situation.
