"INNOVATION"

...is at the tip of everyon'es tongue in the rail industry now. A word that was disregarded and thrown away 24 months ago is now the hottest thing since the Stephenson Rocket.

Unfortunately, despite being so heavily used, so little is actually being done in the industry, and what little activity there is second rate compared to other markets and industries.

We're not here to answer how we've ended up here in this situation.

WE'RE HERE TO GET OUT OF IT...

A lot is made of the word ‘innovation’, and much like ‘disrupt’ it is a word that features strongly on websites, business strategies and marketing campaigns worldwide. These words have become a phenomenon unto themselves. Overapplication threatens to reduce both to their lowest common denominators, as their meaning and the cultural narrative they represent becomes all the more ambiguous.
